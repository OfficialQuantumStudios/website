DMCA Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-21 (YYYY-MM-DD)

We respect the intellectual property rights of others and expect our users to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (“DMCA”), we will respond promptly to claims of copyright infringement on our platform when they are properly reported to us.
This DMCA Policy outlines how copyright owners may submit a takedown notice and how we respond to such notices.

1. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been copied in a way that constitutes infringement and is accessible on our service, please submit a written DMCA takedown notice to our designated agent.
Upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice, we will promptly remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing material and may notify the user who posted it. (if applicable)

2. DMCA Takedown Notice Requirements

To be considered a valid takedown request, you must include all the following information:
  1. Your physical or electronic signature.
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
    3. - If multiple works are involved, provide a representative list.
  3. Identification of where on our services the material that is claimed to be infringing is.
    4. - Include URLs or specific location information sufficient for us to locate the material.
  4. A statement of good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  6. Contact information at least including a contact email address.
Send all the following information to our DMCA email address, dmca@qsnetwork.dev, to submit a takedown request.

3. Modifications

We reserve the right to update or modify our DMCA Policy at any time. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date at the top of the document.
